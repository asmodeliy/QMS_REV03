🚀 APQP overview

APQP

Plan and track Advanced Product Quality Planning milestones with clear owners and due dates.

Timeline
  • Select: Program + gate (Plan/Design/Validate/Launch)
  • Track: Due dates, owners, status
  • Evidence: Attach reports (descriptive names)

🗂️ Phases

  • Plan & Define: Voice of customer, feasibility, program charter.
  • Product Design: DFMEA, specs, drawing releases, prototype builds.
  • Process Design: PFMEA, control plan drafts, tooling readiness.
  • Validate: Run@rate, MSA/GRR, capability (Cpk/Ppk) evidence.
  • Launch & Feedback: PPAP, lessons learned, change management.

📑 Deliverables & owners

Assignments

Each card lists owner and due date. Keep remarks brief and date-stamped for traceability.

Evidence

Upload key artifacts: DFMEA/PFMEA, control plans, MSA, capability reports, PPAP packages.

Reviews

Use status to flag review needs; summarize decisions after each gate meeting.

❓ FAQ

  • Files: PDF/Office; zip large files
  • New fields: Contact admin (gate + deliverable type)
  • Export: Screenshot boards before reviews
